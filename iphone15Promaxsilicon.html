Product Details

Designed by Apple to complement iPhone 16 Plus, the Silicone Case with MagSafe is a delightful way to protect your iPhone. And on the inside, there’s a soft microfiber lining for even more protection. This case works seamlessly with Camera Control. Like every Apple-designed case, it undergoes thousands of hours of testing throughout the design and manufacturing process.
